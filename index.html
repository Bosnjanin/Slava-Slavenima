About Us

The Monash Slav Society is an inclusive student club dedicated to the celebration and education of Slavic identity and culture.

Whether you come from Serbia, Slovakia, Russia, Poland, Ukraine, Bosnia, Croatia, Bulgaria, Czechia, Slovenia, Macedonia, Montenegro, or simply love Slavic heritage, you are welcome here.

Our mission is to build a strong, vibrant community where Slavic students and allies can connect, share, and grow.

What We Do

  • Events: Celebrate traditional Slavic holidays, music, food, and dance.
  • Talks & Discussions: Explore Slavic history, politics, and languages through guest speakers and community forums.
  • Language & Learning: Access resources and conversation groups to practice Slavic languages.
  • Community: Connect with Slavs from across Monash and beyond, fostering friendships and solidarity.
